    1. Guaranteed immediate income, with the latest order fulfillment and earnings starting the following day.
    2. Multiple revenue streams are available:
      a. Income from personally sourcing and delivering products.
      b. Income from building and managing your own sourcing team.
      c. Income from independently selling training courses.
      d. Income from co-selling training courses in partnership with us.
      e. Income from collaborating with us on the trade platform, officially entering large-scale trading opportunities.

    Japanese and Korean purchasing agent courses, as well as China cross-border sourcing programs, primarily teach you how to buy goods and resell them independently. In contrast, our small-scale trade training focuses on teaching you where to source products directly and how to efficiently distribute them through established sales channels. The two approaches differ significantly and represent entirely different business models.

    Most courses on the market, 99% of them, focus solely on teaching you the skills, leaving you to find your own path to monetization. In contrast, we not only teach you the skills but also provide direct pathways to generate income, ensuring that you can start earning immediately—something other courses cannot guarantee.

    Through this model, our team has successfully developed five distinct revenue streams and handles a substantial volume of goods. Teaching additional participants will not diminish our income, and we are even more committed to expanding our training channels to further increase overall profitability.

    Companions are not permitted to join. To protect our organization’s revenue structure and safeguard the long-term interests of future participants, we do not offer complimentary seats. Additionally, you will be responsible for purchasing your own flight tickets and handling your own travel documents, which further minimizes potential concerns.
